Welcome to Boundary Court Motor Inn

Welcome to Boundary Court Motor Inn, Hamilton, conveniently located on Boundary Road between the Central City and Claudelands Park and Events Centre (5 mins walk). Near University of Waikato and Science Innovation Park. Our comfortable, fresh, well designed suites will make your stay with us enjoyable and relaxing. A mere stroll over the Mighty Waikato River connects you to the heart of Hamilton City. Are you the fan of Movie "THE HOBBIT: AN UNEXPECTED JOURNEY"?  We just about one hour drive to Hobbit Town (Matamata).
